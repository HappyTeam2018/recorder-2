Take snapshot from your webcam

For program explanation visit:
https://cooltrickshome.blogspot.in/2016/11/take-snapshot-from-webcam-using-java-in.html

Make sure to add below pom dependency:
   <dependency>  
        <groupId>com.github.sarxos</groupId>  
        <artifactId>webcam-capture</artifactId>  
        <version>0.3.10</version>  
        <scope>test</scope>  
   </dependency>
